USAGE
-----
To use a sound file, call: Spring.PlaySoundFile("path", volume)
Example: Spring.PlaySoundFile("sounds/alarm.wav", 0.7)

RECOMMENDED SOUNDS FOR QUEEN DEATH DISPLAY
-------------------------------------------
- sounds/alarm.wav (alarm sound)
- sounds/ui/warning1.wav (warning sound)
- sounds/ui/warning2.wav (alternative warning)
- sounds/beep6.wav (beep notification)
- sounds/ui/mappoint.wav (map marker sound)

Note: Some paths may work with capital "S" (Sounds/...) if lowercase doesn't work.
The widget will try both automatically.
